Thank you very much for your interest in volunteering for YOUtopia 2015! Our mutual participation as volunteers helps make YOUtopia the magical place it is. EVERYONE who attends YOUtopia volunteers in some way, whether by assisting their theme camp, helping fellow participants, building art, or contributing their talents to the event.

All participants are encouraged to come by the volunteer headquarters at the V Spot at any time to find a team of superheroes who need your help, but please submit this application *only* if you are confident in your ability to commit to volunteering your helping hands and heart for the specific teams designated below for a total of between 16-20 hours.

If you cannot commit to 16-20 hours of being incredibly awesome, please swing through the V Spot on site whenever you are able, contact us at volunteer@sdyoutopia.com, join us in the YOUtopia Volunteers Facebook group, or talk to a department head directly to find the best fit for your amazing talents.
